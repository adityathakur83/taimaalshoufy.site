Modern Classical Luxury

An ivory & champagne-gold classical villa.

This interior design follows a modern classical luxury concept. I used a soft ivory colour palette with champagne gold accents to create an elegant and bright villa atmosphere. The design includes classical wall mouldings, fluted columns, decorative capitals, gypsum cornices, crystal chandeliers, wall sconces, polished marble flooring, mirrors, and luxury fabric furniture.

The main design idea is to create a grand and timeless interior with rich classical details, while keeping the space light and comfortable. The arches, wall panels, gold trims, chandeliers, and marble feature walls give the space a luxurious identity, while the mirrors, glass planter, and soft neutral colours keep it fresh and modern. Overall, the design represents elegance, symmetry, luxury, and refined classical details.

Design Concept

A modern luxury interior built around a central indoor garden.

Inspired by natural materials and indoor greenery — light travertine stone, warm wood veneer, glass railing, soft beige fabrics, dark metal trims and hidden warm lighting in careful balance. The double-height volume gives the space a strong architectural character, layered pendant lights emphasise the vertical, and a strong connection is drawn between architecture, landscape and interior design.
