My Approach

From the Levant
to the Gulf.

I studied architecture for five years in Syria, where design was taught as a discipline of both thinking and making. That foundation — structural, material, proportional — informs every space I create today.

Since relocating to Abu Dhabi I have designed across residential interiors, commercial fit-outs, event stages and exhibition stands. My time at Rainlight Events Company sharpened my instinct for high-pressure, high-visibility projects — including on-site video editing at Sheikh Zayed Festival 2024. At Reflection Engineering Consultancy, I now translate client briefs into considered spaces rendered in full 3D before construction begins.

I work directly with each client, communicate fluently in Arabic and English, and do not outsource the design thinking.
