What I Do

From first sketch
to final finish.

Architecture-informed interior design — grounded in structure, refined through 3D, delivered to schedule.

01

Interior Design

+

Creative spatial concepts built around your lifestyle, budget and the specific qualities of each room — from furniture placement and material selection to lighting schemes that transform a space hour by hour.

02

Architecture & Fit-Out

+

Full architectural thinking applied to interiors: structural awareness, AutoCAD documentation, BIM coordination and sustainable detailing calibrated for UAE building standards and climate.

03

3D Visualisation

+

Photorealistic renders and animated walkthroughs in 3Ds Max, SketchUp and ArchiCAD — so clients see, approve and refine the final result before a single tile is laid.

04

Event & Exhibition Design

+

Tendering-ready 3D concepts for stages, pavilions and exhibition stands, delivered under event-industry timelines. Experience includes Sheikh Zayed Festival 2024.

05

BOQ & Costing

+

Itemised bills of quantities, supplier coordination and project management — keeping fit-outs on budget from first estimate to final handover.

06

End-to-End Delivery

+

A single point of contact from initial brief to project handover. Direct client communication in Arabic and English, structured revisions and timely delivery aligned with Gulf market standards.
